# Exercise 04 — Share Pool Review

**Difficulty:** Intermediate

## Objective

Review an Ether-denominated share pool. Evaluate deposit and withdrawal accounting across multiple users, direct transfers, rounding behavior, and changing pool balances.

## Prerequisites

- Vault share accounting
- Integer division and rounding
- Invariants
- Fuzz testing
- Donation and balance-manipulation threat models

## Scope

- `SharePool.sol`

## Required Deliverables

- Accounting model and intended invariants
- Vulnerability finding or defensible no-finding conclusion
- Fuzz or invariant test
- Remediation and regression test
- Professional finding report

Do not use public solutions before submitting your first attempt.
